## Lab 1: Pipetting & Spectrophotometry Basics
<br>o	Pipette Calibration: Test the accuracy of automatic (1 ml) and glass (10 ml) pipettes by weighing delivered water volumes.
<br>o	Absorption Spectrum: Determine the $\lambda_{max}$ (wavelength of maximum absorption) for Methylene Blue dye by measuring absorbance across 500–700 nm.
<br>o	Calibration Curve: Prepare a dilution series of a standard dye (0–5 $\mu g/ml$), measure absorbance at $\lambda_{max}$, and plot a calibration curve to find the concentration of an unknown solution.
